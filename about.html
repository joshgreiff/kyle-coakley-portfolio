Hey there!

I'm Kyle

I grew up in Grove City, Ohio with my parents and three brothers, we all had names that started with the letter “K” so it was confusing for the parents. I am a huge sports fan, I root for my Cleveland Browns and anything Ohio State related. In my high school/college days, I was involved in running sports such as track and cross country and I still enjoy doing 5K’s and mud runs.

I went to Kent State University where I dreamed of being a General Manager at a restaurant or golf club. I quickly realized that the private club world was not my calling but I thoroughly enjoyed all the people interaction experience that I received from working at Chick Fil a, casual restaurants, fine dining, bartender gigs and golf clubs. I still find myself bartending once a week at a tequila bar, I just cannot bring myself away just yet from the lively atmosphere.

I now spend my days in Columbus, Ohio where most of my family resides, we are all into fitness so I love spending time exercising and being outside. I have a pet cat named Odin, my best friend, and I enjoy going for walks with him in my backpack. Thanks for getting to know me and I hope we work together soon.
