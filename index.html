Hi and welcome to my page, My name is Kyle Coakley and I am here to fulfill and exceed your expectations.

What Do I Bring To the Table?

I work for BBI Logistics, a 3PL company that was certified as one of the top 50 places to work in Columbus, Ohio as well as being voted the fastest growing company in Columbus, Ohio for 2021. I can offer you carriers in all 48 contiguous states as well as Canada. I can help you with any lane, even the nasty mult drops. I specialize in handling full truck load shipments while also dealing with a variety of other services such as, LTL, intermodal, specialized equipment, and expediting shipments. My goal is to help you find the most cost-effective and reliable carrier while also maximizing load potential for my carriers that I have built great relationships with. One thing you will come to find out about myself is that I don’t quit. My phone is always on, even when the traditional work day ends at 5:00 PM, I will still be able to help and make myself available for you and for the carrier hauling your cargo. I am young and hungry and will be in this industry for a long time and would love to make long lasting work relationships. If you want to hear from me in person, I am more than willing to hop in my car and meet you and your team. I would also love to tour your facility!
